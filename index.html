Welcome to our Bakery

Welcome to Prisca's Bakery & Confectionary, a modern Ugandan-style bakery and confectionary that celebrates the flavors and traditions of the ugandan culture through food. We offer a variety of pastries, breads, snacks and birthday cakes. We strive to support local vendors whenever possible by purchasing their products as well.

Excellent hospitality is what we strive for: from our ordering process, to the production of our baked goods, and their delivery, we are always focused on making our clients feel comfortable and well taken care of.

Born during a time of pandemic, Prisca's Bakery & Confectionary celebrates the experiences that make us human like sharing delicious snacks with our friends and family. We invite you to visit the shop where you can choose from a variety of fresh and handmade menu options.
